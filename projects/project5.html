Agitator Blade Design & Manufacturing Development

Developed a fully defined CAD model and engineering drawing from an incomplete and minimally detailed sketch of an agitator blade. Missing geometry and critical features were interpreted and reconstructed using engineering judgment, functional requirements, and manufacturing considerations. The design was communicated to a machinist for production, and iterative feedback was incorporated to refine features for manufacturability, tolerancing, and overall performance. The final result was a production-ready design that bridged the gap between conceptual input and a functional, manufacturable component. This project highlights the ability to translate vague concepts into precise, manufacturable solutions through iteration and cross-disciplinary collaboration.
