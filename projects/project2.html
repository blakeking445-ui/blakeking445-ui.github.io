Toolpath Programming

Designed and developed precision CAM toolpaths in Fusion 360 to manufacture a replacement tab used to secure an agitator blade to a rotating shaft. The project involved modelling the component, generating optimized machining strategies, and producing production-ready G-code directly within Fusion 360. Multiple iterations were machined and evaluated on the production line to ensure consistent fit, durability, and manufacturability. The final process successfully translated a digital design into a reliable, repeatable manufacturing workflow suitable for small-batch production.
