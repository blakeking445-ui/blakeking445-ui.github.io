Project 1 · Reverse engineering

Designed and prototyped a custom 3D-printed fuel door mount to replace a broken factory part. The component was reverse-engineered using a 3D scan of the original geometry, then modelled in Fusion 360 to match the vehicle’s surface contours precisely. Multiple iterations were printed and tested to refine fit, strength, and alignment, resulting in a functional replacement part tailored for real-world use.
